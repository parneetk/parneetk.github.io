Welcome to my website!

I am a PhD candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Rutgers University. I am interested in the fields of computer vision, machine learning and pattern recognition. Currently, I am working with Dr. Kristin J. Dana to study the link between skin appearance and skin microbiome. Skin appearance is modeled using computational models from high resolution multimodal images. We are developing high dimensional clustering algorithms for  appearance-microbiome as well as traditional computer vision heterogeneous datasets. This project is funded by Johnson & Johnson.
