STEM week at CCLC

This week I spent some time with pre-schoolers and pre-K students doing some fun activities. We did ice-fishing and made toys vanish with polarizers. Their favorite was the holographic projection which they couldn’t touch! We designed towers with water cups and ice cream sticks. Each student also designed an emoji with her/his favorite profession at Made with Code.

This event was sponsored by a Women Techmakers outreach grant.
